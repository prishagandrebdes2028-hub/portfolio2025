My Work

Research Article

Researched about vegetable vendors on Station Road, Matunga East. I personally interviewed many of these vendors and understood their long-standing history, a day in their life, how they maintain customer relationships, modern adaptability, cultural significance and how they source their vegetables. I wrote a research article with the title "Family Businesses in Matunga"...

Brand Building

Created a new brand called “Tata Crunch” which is an upcoming healthy snacking brand. This was a brand conjugation between Tata cliq and Haldiram’s. These products will be available on the Tata Cliq app and is targeted towards a health conscious, young audience.

Brochure Making

As a part of an assignment this tri-fold brochure was designed to showcase Oticon's advanced hearing aids, emphasizing features like brain support, natural sound, and phone streaming. The layout includes user testimonials, a visual comparison chart, and real-ear imagery to enhance credibility. A QR code simplifies bookings and encourages hearing tests.The design uses Oticon’s signature magenta brand color throughout, reinforcing brand identity while maintaining a clean, accessible look.

Poster Making

Poster from the Baroque era where I designed the label by getting inspired by motifs and designs from that period. I have used gilded motifs for the background and cherubs as the main design, hence it’s called “Gilded Cherub”. Later I turned it into a poster.
